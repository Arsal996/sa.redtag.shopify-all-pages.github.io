    • Deck up Your Baby Boy

    Styling clothing is an art. Here are some easy style hacks for you to make your kid look charming with Redtag’s boys’ clothing items.

  • Infants: Comfort is the most important thing that you must consider when it comes to dressing infants. All infant clothes already look extremely cute, therefore, you need not put in any extra effort. Just ensure that the outfit is soft and gentle on your baby’s skin and causes no irritation. With appropriate outfits, do not forget cute boys’ shoes to keep their feet warm.
  • Preschoolers: School-going kids have a slighly eventful life. A lot of birthday parties, outings and playdates are just some reasons you may want your little one to appear well-dressed. For a birthday party or a playdate, you can dress your son in denim shorts and a contrasting T-shirt with suspenders. You can also add accessories such as a hat. For footwear, opt for cool white sneakers. Finally, have them wear sunglasses for an extremely cute look.
  • Teenagers: Advise your preteens and teens to dress up dapper in a pair of ripped jeans and solid coloured shirts for any special event. They can also wear joggers with a printed T-shirt and casual shoes for a cool everyday look. You can also give them some delightful boys’ fragrances to smell great throughout the day.
